Our Products

Paper and Leather

Crafted from paper and leather, these items range from decorative art to functional objects like journals, bags, and intricate sculptures.

Textile

Using various fabrics and fibers, textile crafts include weaving, embroidery, and dyeing techniques to create items like clothing, rugs, and tapestries.

Wood and Bamboo

Made from natural materials like wood and bamboo, these crafts include furniture, utensils, and decorative items, often reflecting intricate carvings and regional styles.

Ceramic and Pottery

Shaped from clay and then fired, ceramic and pottery crafts include dishes, vases, and decorative pieces, celebrated for their durability and unique glazing techniques.

Embroidery

a decorative technique that involves stitching patterns on fabric using a needle and thread. It is often used to enhance the beauty of textiles, creating intricate and elaborate designs. Embroidery can vary in complexity, with some pieces requiring months or even years to complete.
